About SustainOpedia

SustainOpedia is a next-generation platform for Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) and sustainability intelligence. Our mission is to make environmental data accessible, actionable, and understandable for everyone—from industry professionals to students and policymakers.

AI-Powered LCA Chat

Our platform combines a comprehensive Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) knowledge base with advanced AI capabilities to deliver instant, reliable insights. Designed as an interactive chat system, it empowers users to ask complex LCA-related questions and receive clear, data-driven answers in real time. By uniting expert knowledge with conversational AI, the platform streamlines sustainability analysis and makes LCA accessible to professionals, researchers, and organizations seeking smarter decision-making.

IFRS S2 Climate Disclosure Engine

Our platform integrates climate science, geospatial analytics, and AI narrative synthesis into a seamless three-layer engine. The first layer applies SSP1-SSP5 scenarios to forecast climate hazards. The second layer maps infrastructure risks through advanced geospatial modeling. The third layer employs large language models to generate structured narratives that directly conform to IFRS S2 disclosure requirements. This end-to-end pipeline eliminates manual intervention, transforming raw climate inputs into regulator-ready disclosure documents with full automation.

Schema-Based Audit-Ready Validation

To ensure absolute compliance, every report undergoes schema-driven validation against the IFRS S2 framework. This automated mechanism enforces strict alignment with standardized data templates, guaranteeing that each disclosure is 100% consistent with regulatory expectations. The result is an audit-ready output that can be submitted directly to third-party reviewers, meeting professional assurance standards without additional manual checks.
