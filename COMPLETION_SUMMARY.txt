## 🔧 Technical Implementation

### Backend Architecture
```
Express.js Server
├── Authentication Routes
│   ├── POST /api/auth/register
│   └── POST /api/auth/login
├── Chat Routes
│   ├── GET /api/chat-histories
│   └── POST /api/chat-histories
├── Records Routes
│   ├── GET /api/lca-records
│   ├── POST /api/lca-records
│   └── DELETE /api/lca-records/:id
└── Health Route
    └── GET /api/health

MongoDB Collections
├── users (User accounts)
├── chathistories (Conversations)
└── lcarecords (LCA calculations)
```

├── Registration Form
